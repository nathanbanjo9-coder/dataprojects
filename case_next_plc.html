Business Goal

Project Summary

Next Plc is one of the UK's largest fashion and homeware retailers operating across in-store, online, Next App and Next Directory channels. This project simulates the analytical environment of a BI Analyst at Next, building a complete end to end reporting solution covering financial performance, returns analysis, regional intelligence and revenue forecasting across 800,000 synthetic transactions from 2022 to 2024.

Key Questions

  • Which product categories and channels drive the most revenue and margin?
  • What is the true cost of returns and where is margin leaking?
  • How does discount rate impact gross profit across categories?
  • Which regions are outperforming and underperforming?
  • What does the revenue forecast look like for the next 6 months?

Dataset

  • 800,000 synthetic retail orders across 5 categories and 4 channels
  • 80,000 customers, 2,000 products, 120 stores, 10 UK regions
  • 3 years of data from January 2022 to December 2024
  • Generated using Python (pandas, faker, numpy)

Analysis Structure

How the Analysis Was Built

Full multi-tool approach with distinct phases for data modelling, interactive reporting, scenario planning, and predictive forecasting.

Block 1 — PostgreSQL: SQL Foundation
DATA MODELING · ANALYSIS
  • 800,000 row dataset across 5 tables — orders, returns, products, customers, stores
  • 22 queries across 4 layers — validation, commercial KPIs, advanced analytics, window functions
  • RANK(), LAG(), PARTITION BY, CTEs, stored procedures and cross joins
  • Data validation checks for nulls, duplicates and revenue integrity
  • Regional performance scorecard with multi-metric simultaneous ranking
Block 2 — Power BI: Interactive Dashboard
REPORTING · DAX · RLS
  • Page 1 Executive Overview — KPI cards, revenue by channel, revenue by category, monthly trend
  • Page 2 Sales Performance — matrix, top 10 products, YoY growth, regional bar chart
  • Page 3 Returns and Margin — return rate trend, return reasons, scatter chart, insights panel
  • Row Level Security for Regional Manager and Executive roles
  • 12 DAX measures including SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR, TOTALYTD, DIVIDE and CALCULATE
Block 3 — Excel: Financial Workbook
KPI MODELLING · SCENARIOS
  • RAW_DATA sheet — 800,000 rows loaded via Power Query
  • KPI Summary — 13 headline metrics using SUMIF, COUNTIF, SUMPRODUCT
  • Pivot Dashboard — 3 connected pivot tables with category, year and channel slicers
  • Scenario Model — interactive what-if discount impact model with input/output cells
Block 4 — Python: Analytical Charts
FORECASTING · VISUALS
  • 6 charts using matplotlib and scikit-learn
  • Monthly revenue trend with shaded area fill
  • Revenue by category + return rate with average line
  • Revenue by channel pie chart and YoY growth bar chart
  • Linear regression forecast 6 months into 2025
Block 5 — Azure: Cloud Architecture
PIPELINE ARCHITECTURE
  • Enterprise data pipeline diagram
  • Sources: POS, e-commerce, directory, app
  • Ingestion: Azure Data Factory
  • Storage: ADLS Gen2, Azure SQL Database
  • Processing: Synapse + Databricks; reporting: Power BI Service

Enterprise Data Pipeline Flow

This data architecture diagram shows how a company like Next Plc moves data from source systems (POS, e-commerce, app directory) through Azure services and into reporting solutions. It represents a real enterprise cloud environment design:

Key Findings

Data-Driven Insights

Five principal insights from the end-to-end analysis.

01 Revenue Growth Profile

Revenue stable at £206.5M but growth is decelerating — Womenswear declined 1.6% in 2022-2023 before recovering to 2.0% growth in 2023-2024.

02 Channel Mix

Channel mix is almost perfectly equal at 25% across all four channels suggesting digital channels may be underperforming their growth potential.

03 Returns Risk

Returns represent a £24.9M revenue risk with Better price elsewhere cited in 17% of all returns — a direct pricing competitiveness signal.

04 Margin Pressure

Gross margin holds at 57.69% but £17.5M annual discount impact creates material profit risk if promotional strategy is not carefully managed.

05 Regional Performance

North West leads regional revenue at £21M with broadly even national distribution across all 10 regions.

Recommendations

Action Plan

Five tactical recommendations aligned to business questions and findings.

01 Womenswear Subcategory Review

Investigate Womenswear subcategory decline through product level analysis and competitor benchmarking.

02 Dynamic Pricing

Implement dynamic pricing review to address the 17% of returns citing better price elsewhere.

03 Digital Channel Investment

Accelerate Next App and Next Directory investment to shift revenue toward higher margin digital channels.

04 Margin Control Dashboard

Introduce monthly margin monitoring dashboard with early warning thresholds at 55% gross margin.

05 Returns Reduction Program

Build a returns reduction programme starting with Home category root cause analysis by product, courier and region.

Limitations

Scope Constraints

Important analytical boundaries and validation notes.

Synthetic Dataset

Synthetic dataset — real operational data would require validation against live POS and ERP systems.

Margin Depth

No margin data at product level — analysis uses category level gross margin only.

Customer Lifetime Value

No customer lifetime value modelling — repeat purchase behaviour not tracked at cohort level.

Conceptual Architecture

Azure architecture is conceptual — not a live deployed pipeline.
